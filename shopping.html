Haleiwa Marketplace

If the islands of Hawai‘i are a beach lover’s paradise, then the O‘ahu town of Hale‘iwa is the shopper’s version of consumer Nirvana.

But what might be news to many is that the shopping in this unique enclave is also a magnetic attraction.

For a small town, Hale‘iwa boasts a wide range of retail options. The stores here are mostly housed in aesthetically adorable plantation-era buildings within two main complexes.

The North Shore Marketplace and Hale‘iwa Town Center run the retail gamut from surf gear shops to art galleries, and offer goods for purchase like unique clothing, jewelry, souvenirs and more.
