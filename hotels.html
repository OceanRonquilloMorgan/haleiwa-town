Places to Stay in Haleiwa

Turtle Bay Resort


The resort consists of 375 luxury guest rooms, 26 suites, 42 beach cottages and luxury ocean villas with scenic views of the Pacific Ocean.

The resort offers promotional packages and monthly discount rates starting at around $200.00 a night for a room.


Amentities:

Golf Course

Horseback Riding

Surfing Lessons

Helicopter Tours


Budget:

High

Ke'Iki Bungalows


Located at Ke Iki Beach, these are spacious one and two bedroom units with full kitchen, telephones & TV, barbecues, picnic tables, and hammocks.

Choose ocean front or garden view units just steps from the beach.


Amentities:

Ocean View

Mountain View

Easy Beach Access

Budget:

Medium

Camp Mokule'ia


Features spacious meeting rooms, double-occupancy rooms with spectacular ocean views, private and semi-private bathrooms, and delicious healthy home-cooked meals served cafeteria style.

Campers spend one to six weeks in facilities with two trained counselors per cabin unit.


Amentities:

Reef Exploration

Snorkeling

Ropes Course

Budget:

Affordable
